HIDDEN CLOUD VILLAGE

Hidden Cloud Village, literally meaning: Village Hidden by Clouds) is the hidden village of the Land of Lightning and was founded by the First Raikage.
As the village of one of the Five Great Shinobi Countries, Kumogakure has a Kage as its leader known as the Raikage, of which there have been five in its history.
The village is located in a range of tall mountains, and is literally hidden in the clouds.
The Raikage works in a large blue structure built into the tallest mountain.

The standard attire for Kumo shinobi consists of a long, grey top which gathers just at the waist to give a sash-like appearance, with a matching coloured bottom.
Over this they wear white, one-strapped flak jackets and arm as well as shin-guards.
Many Kumogakure ninja use Lightning Release techniques.
