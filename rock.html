HIDDEN STONE VILLAGE

Hidden Stone Village, literally meaning: Village Hidden by Rocks is the hidden village of the Land of Earth.
As the village of one of the Five Great Shinobi Countries, Iwagakure has a Kage as its leader known as the Tsuchikage, of which there have been four in its history.
The rocky mountain ranges that surround the village provide a natural stronghold that it is very proud of.
The village's infrastructure is built from much of the surrounding rock and stone, shaped into tower-like structures that are inter-connected by a network of bridges.
The Tsuchikage's residence seems to be the tallest structure with a cone-shaped roof and bears the kanji for "Earth" (土, Tsuchi) on it.

The standard attire for Iwa shinobi consists of red outfit — which can either have one, or both sleeves — a lapel which is usually found on the side without a sleeve and a brown flak jacket — which also may, or may not have a pouch attached to it.
They also tend to wear mesh armour around their ankles.
Many Iwagakure ninja use Earth Release techniques.
Iwagakure is well-known for the rock-hard attitude of its shinobi; as soon as the Tsuchikage gives an order, the Iwa-nin follow it without hesitation, even if it means death.
